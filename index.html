Featured Poem

Whispers of Dawn

In the quiet hours before the world awakes,
When shadows dance with morning light,
The earth holds secrets that daybreak makes,
And dreams dissolve into sight.

Each dewdrop holds a story untold,
Of nights that passed in silent grace,
While stars above, both young and old,
Watched over this sacred space.
